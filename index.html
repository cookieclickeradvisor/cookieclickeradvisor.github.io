Frequently Asked Questions

How does Cookie Clicker Advisor work?

Cookie Clicker Advisor analyzes your game in real-time and provides smart recommendations based on your current game state, upgrades, and achievements. It helps you make optimal decisions to maximize your cookie production.

Is the extension free to use?

Yes! Cookie Clicker Advisor is completely free to use. Simply install it from the Chrome Web Store and start optimizing your gameplay immediately.

Will this affect my game progress?

No, Cookie Clicker Advisor is designed to be non-intrusive. It only provides recommendations and tracking - it doesn't modify your game or save data in any way.
